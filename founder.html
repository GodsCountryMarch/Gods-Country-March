Meet the Founder

This movement was born out of a simple conviction: Idaho can lead with courage and kindness, standing under the Cross while serving our neighbors.

Your Name is the founder of God’s Country March. After the assassination of Charlie Kirk and witnessing the unity and hope of Idaho’s faithful community, the vision formed to gather people for prayer, worship, and public witness — and to serve families through tangible outreach.

God’s Country March exists to honor Christ, thank God for our freedoms, and love our neighbors well. We are picking up where Charlie left off, ensuring that his vision for America does not die with him. We believe a peaceful people can be powerful people — especially when we show up together.

“My heart is to see Idaho model faith and courage — not to yell, but to stand — and to build communities of prayer and service that continue long after any one event.”
