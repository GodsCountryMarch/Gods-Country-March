GOD’S COUNTRY MARCH

Uniting Idaho in faith, freedom, and community

Tue, Oct 14, 2025 • 6:00 PM • Idaho State Capitol Steps

Idaho’s first God’s Country March. Let’s make Idaho the leader in what a God-centered country looks like. Honor the mission that Charlie started. Add your voice to the Capitol steps on October 14th. This is a movement of faith, and a stand against those who would try to silence us.

What is the God’s Country March?

This is a public witness of faith and freedom in Idaho. We gather to take a stand and celebrate the freedoms God has given each person in our nation. We honor Christ, and we honor Charlie. We are God’s Country. We are Charlie.

Faith

Prayer, worship, and public witness to Christ.

Freedom

Celebrating the freedoms God has given our nation.

Community

Serving families and neighbors across Idaho.
