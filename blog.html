Welcome, Patriot — Christ is King

• God’s Country March

If you’re here, you’re probably a patriot and a Christian who is ready to stand up and boldly proclaim that Christ is King. We’re done being pushed around. We’re done being silent. This movement is for those who will not bow to fear or drift with the culture.

The God’s Country March is a public witness—faith in the open, love for neighbor, and courage in the streets. We’re gathering for Charlie, for conviction and for hope. We believe that when believers stand together, God can awaken hearts and change the future of our communities.

So here’s the call: link arms with us. Commit to proclaim loudly and boldy that Idaho is God's Country. Invite your family and friends. Bring your voice, energy and your courage. Let the Cross be our banner and the Capitol steps our pulpit. Silence is surrender, and we refuse to surrender what God has given.

This is your moment. Step into it. Speak truth with grace. Lift high the name of Jesus in the public square. Let Idaho lead with faith, freedom, and unashamed conviction.

See you on the steps.

— A fellow Patriot and Christian
