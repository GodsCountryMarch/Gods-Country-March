Mission & Vision

We exist to publicly honor Christ, celebrate the freedoms God has given our nation, and serve families in Idaho through prayer, outreach, and discipleship.

Faith

Prayer, worship, and public witness to Christ.

Freedom

Thanking God for our liberties and stewarding them well.

Community

Serving neighbors across Idaho in practical ways.

Our Approach

  • Peaceful, prayerful gatherings that unify across differences
  • Ongoing outreach: food drives, support for families, prayer nights
  • Discipleship resources, workshops, and small groups
  • Partnerships with local churches and community leaders

What’s Next

  • Regular prayer gatherings in multiple Idaho cities
  • Seasonal outreach events to meet community needs
  • Training for local teams to organize and serve
  • Statewide day of faith and service
