Mission & Vision

Uniting Idaho in faith, freedom, and community.

Our Mission

We march because silence is surrender. God’s Country March is a declaration that Christ is not a tame Sunday service, but the living King who overturns corruption, confronts sin, and calls His people to rise. Our mission is to link arms, lift our voices, and show a watching world that faith is not hiding in the shadows — it’s alive, loud, and unshaken.

Declaration “Faith is not hiding in the shadows — it’s alive, loud, and unshaken.”

Our Vision

We see a Church that refuses to bow to culture or fear, but stands in bold defiance with the same fire that drove Jesus to flip tables. God’s Country March is the spark of a movement where believers walk unashamed through the streets, declaring revival and truth. Our vision is a generation awakened, a nation stirred, and a faith so fierce it can’t be silenced.
