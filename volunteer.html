Volunteer

Link arms with us. There’s a place for every gift — prayer, outreach, setup, first aid, media, and more.

Ways to Serve

Prayer Team

Cover the gathering in prayer, pray with attendees, and be a peaceful presence.

Outreach & Flyering

Share invites online and locally. Table at churches or community spots.

Logistics & Setup

Help with setup/teardown, signage, staging, cables, and equipment.

Peacekeepers

Calm, friendly volunteers who help with directions and crowd flow.

Media & Content

Shoot photos/video, capture testimonies, or help with social media.

First Aid

Licensed/experienced helpers for minor needs; bring basic supplies if you can.

Hospitality

Welcome table, water handout, family support, and accessibility help.

Drivers

Help shuttle, haul gear, or transport supplies (truck/van helpful).
